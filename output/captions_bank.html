Ukrainian Boxing Technique Explained
Video post · Technical / authority
A lot of people ask us what makes Ukrainian-style boxing different from what you'd learn at most boxing clubs. Here's the honest answer. Most gyms teach a standard upright stance — it's fine for fitness boxing and beginner sparring. But it leaves you exposed when the pace increases. Ukrainian boxing prioritises defensive structure: a tighter shell, weight distribution that allows movement in any direction, and combinations that flow from a protected position rather than an open one. It's not flashy. It's disciplined, technical, and genuinely effective — which is exactly why our coaches teach it.
