## Post 3: 5 Mistakes Beginners Make in Their First Boxing Class
**Platforms:** Instagram Reel + TikTok + Facebook
**Audience:** Segment A — fitness seekers (pre sign-up, slightly nervous)
**Emotional trigger:** Pain (fear of embarrassment) → relief (normalised)
**Format:** Reel 30–45s (quick demos of each mistake — coach demonstrates)

---

### HOW TO FILM (iPhone — under 15 mins)
Film 5 quick 3–4 second clips. Coach demonstrates each "mistake" — exaggerate slightly for visual clarity. Keep it light and slightly humorous (builds relatability).

1. Looking at your own hands instead of the target (glancing down mid-combo)
2. Crossing your feet when moving (over-stepping)
3. Dropping the guard after every punch (hands falling after the jab)
4. Holding breath during combinations (exaggerate — puff out cheeks)
5. Trying to hit too hard too early (swinging wild)

Film each demo from the waist up. Coach talks briefly between clips or a single voiceover is added in editing. Keep energy light — this should make beginners feel "oh that's me, that's totally normal."
