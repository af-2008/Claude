How to Use This Dashboard

What the data shows

Looker Studio is pulling from your Google Analytics 4 account — this tracks visits to your website (heathrowboxingclub.com), not your social media posts directly. It shows: sessions, users, page views, traffic sources, and geographic data.

What to look for weekly

After posting a Reel, check if Direct / Social traffic spikes. A spike means people searched your gym name or clicked a link after seeing your content. Track this weekly to see which content types drive real website visits.

Social media stats

Instagram, TikTok and Facebook don't connect to GA4 — those stats live in their own apps. For IG: go to Professional Dashboard → Insights. For TikTok: Creator Tools → Analytics. Log those numbers in the Performance Analyst workflow (Workflow 08).

Changing the date range

Click the date range selector inside the embedded dashboard above. Set it to Last 28 days for a rolling monthly view. For week-by-week comparison, use Last 7 days and screenshot it each Monday.
